Early Life & Education

Ratan Tata was born on December 28, 1937, in Mumbai, India, into the Tata family, which had established itself as a leader in Indian industry. He was raised by his grandmother, Lady Navajbai Tata, after his parents' separation. Ratan Tata pursued his education at Cornell University, where he studied Architecture and Structural Engineering. Later, he attended Harvard Business School, where he completed an Advanced Management Program.

Career at Tata Group

Ratan Tata joined Tata Group in 1961, starting his career at Tata Steel. Over the years, he gained extensive experience by working on the shop floor. In 1991, he succeeded J.R.D. Tata as the chairman of Tata Group. Under his leadership, Tata expanded globally, acquiring Jaguar Land Rover, Tetley Tea, Corus Steel, and many more companies.
