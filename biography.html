Key Business Achievements

2000 - Acquisition of Tetley

Tata became a global leader in the tea industry.

2007 - Acquisition of Corus Steel

Expanded Tata Steel’s reach in Europe.

2008 - Acquisition of Jaguar Land Rover

Established Tata as a luxury automobile brand.

2009 - Launch of Tata Nano

Introduced the world’s most affordable car.

Biography of Ratan Tata

1937 - Birth

Born on December 28, 1937, in Mumbai, India.

1961 - Joined Tata Group

Started his career at Tata Steel as a trainee.

1991 - Became Chairman

Ratan Tata took over as Chairman of Tata Group.

2012 - Retirement

Retired as Chairman of Tata Sons, leaving a lasting legacy.
